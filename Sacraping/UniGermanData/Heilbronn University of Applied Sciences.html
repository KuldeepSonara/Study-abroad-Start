Description/content

Starting your studies abroad means that changes lie ahead – whether it's the new language, making new friends or achieving success in your academic courses. The ACCESS² programme offers a comprehensive preparation package for prospective engineering or business students.

Programme components include:

  • an intensive German language course: from A2 to B2
  • specialised subjects in engineering (e.g. programming, maths, electrical engineering), business (e.g. marketing, accounting, economics) or informatics in the English language
  • preparation for academic working methods (e.g. presentation skills, academic writing)
  • excursions to companies in the region

Additional support:

  • intercultural buddy programme
  • contact to student assistants regarding questions about campus life
  • weekly consultation hours

Further information on subsequent admission to degree programmes/guaranteed admission

Upon successful completion of the ACCESS² preparation programme, the participants fulfil the language requirements for several Bachelor's programmes at Heilbronn University of Applied Sciences.

In addition, the programme provides an introduction to the following Bachelor's programmes:

Engineering Profile:

  • Electrical Systems Engineering (BEng)
  • Engineering Informatics (BEng)
  • Mechatronics and Robotics (BEng)

Business Profile:

  • International Business – Intercultural Studies

Informatics Profile:

  • Software Engineering

Successful participants can apply directly for the Bachelor's programmes of their choice. Course achievements from the preparation programme can be recognised in the above-mentioned Bachelor's programmes upon request.

Academic admission requirements

The academic aptitude can be proven by submitting a higher education entrance qualification recognised in Germany (applicable to all profiles).

Language requirements

German:

Upon application, participants will be required to provide proof of German language proficiency at completed level A1 of the Common European Framework of Reference for Languages.

English:

While the language course is in German, the technical courses are offered in English. In order to ensure a sufficient level of English in the ACCESS² preparation course, applicants are required to prove that they possess proficient skills in English by means of one of the following certifications:

  • IELTS, minimum 6.0 overall band score
  • TOEFL Internet-based test, minimum 79 points
  • TOEFL paper-based test, minimum 550 points
  • Cambridge CAE (Certificate in Advanced English), minimum C
  • Oxford Test of English (at least 120 points and B2 in all modules)

To ensure a high level of language proficiency, only native speakers of English are exempt from this requirement. Applicants whose school education or first degree were taught in English can be exempted as well. The TOEFL code of Heilbronn University is 2751.
