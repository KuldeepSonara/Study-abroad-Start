Target group

The course is aimed at students of translation studies and German studies.

Description/content

In this course, we will examine various aspects of accessible communication (including audio-visual and intralingual translation) on a theoretical and practical level. We will delve into various strategies and methodologies, such as subtitling, audio description, and the use of easy/simple language. The course is independent of language, so participants from all native language backgrounds can take part.
