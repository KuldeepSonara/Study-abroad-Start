Target group

Young and experienced professionals, graduates, postgraduates, and advanced students who are seeking to enhance their competence in written German for academic purposes (study, doctorate, research, teaching, etc.) or other purposes (journalism, work, etc.)

Description/content

Studying or working in an academic setting in the German-speaking world requires not only a proficient command of the German language but also mastery of the rules and conventions of academic writing in German. This course will teach you techniques and skills that will enable you to write better texts and effectively express your expertise with linguistic precision. From delivering presentations and writing reviews to crafting academic term papers, doctoral theses, or project papers, by the end of the course, you will be proficient in producing well-structured, comprehensible, and engaging academic texts in German.
